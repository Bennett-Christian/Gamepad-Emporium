First time browsing?

Take your time. There is so much to learn when it comes to anything video game related, and controllers are no exception to that. The Gamepad Emporium is here to help facilitate learning about different controllers, but that's not all. Here you can also access a quick and easy solution to finding and purchasing the controller that you are looking for.

Now, it is no secret that there are many people out there who have yet to discover the large world of gaming. With the rapidly consistent growth of gaming communities and the tech sphere in general, it is important that everyone is presented the opportunity to get in the loop and pick up some gaming knowledge of their own. From parents who might want to connect more with their kids, to relatives searching for that perfect birthday gift, there are a plethora of reasons one might want to explore different facets of gaming.

If you are unsure of what kind of controllers you might want, check out the "Modern" section. There you will find the most popular and commonly used controllers that are fully compatible with the newest generation of gaming consoles. On that note, when purchasing controllers, keep in mind that the corresponding video game console will be required in order for each type of controller to function properly. This site operates under the assumption that buyers know they are in the market for controllers specifically, not consoles.

Already familiar?

For those who may already have an idea of what kind of controller they'd like, please feel free to surf around the site at your own discretion.

The "Retro" section houses some classic controllers that some people may already be acquainted with. If not, you never know what might pique your interest, so it is ultimately up to the individual to determine what kind of controller they are truly seeking.
